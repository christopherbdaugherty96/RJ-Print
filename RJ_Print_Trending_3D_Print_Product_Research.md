# RJ Print Trending 3D Print Product Research

Prepared: June 3, 2026

## Executive summary

RJ Print should start with practical, low-IP-risk products: desk organizers, wall caddies, cable holders, plant hangers, car cup-holder inserts, bathroom organizers, SD/USB storage, koozies, lamps, and personalized keychains. These products have broad demand, photograph well, and can be customized locally without touching protected characters or brand logos.

Licensing was treated strictly. Creative Commons says CC BY and CC BY-SA allow commercial use with attribution, while NC licenses are noncommercial only. Cults separates private-use models from commercial-use models, and MakerWorld supports commercial license memberships, but a free download is not automatically a commercial-use file.

## Legal/IP warnings

- Free download does not mean free to sell.
- Avoid NC, NC-SA, NC-ND, Private Use, Personal Use Only, and unclear licenses for commercial products.
- CC BY and CC BY-SA generally allow commercial use, but attribution is required. CC BY-SA also requires ShareAlike for modified/adapted files.
- Avoid protected characters, sports/team logos, brand logos, soda logos, game/movie characters, celebrity likenesses, and vehicle logos.
- For car parts, use compatibility language and avoid implying official affiliation.
- For customer-provided logos, get written confirmation that the customer owns or has permission to use the mark.
- For lamps, use safe off-the-shelf electrical components and avoid unsafe wiring assemblies.
- For food-contact, pet, child, or medical-related items, avoid safety claims unless RJ Print has appropriate testing.

## Final recommendation: what RJ Print should print first

Start with a 10-product launch kit: Basket, Everything Caddy, Car Cup Holder Divider, Plant Hanger, Can Koozie, Workshop Wall Pocket, Cable Holder, Bathroom Organizer Lid, Customizable Keychain, and Spinny Flower. Make 2-3 colorways per item, photograph them well, and test pricing locally before expanding.

RJ Print should also design its own original coaster, soap dish, drawer organizer, and car insert family. Those categories are attractive, but many free files in those spaces are NC/private-use.
