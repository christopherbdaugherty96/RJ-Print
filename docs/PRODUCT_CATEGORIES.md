# RJ Print Product Categories

Status: planning baseline

## Purpose

Define the product categories RJ Print should use on the website, in inventory tracking, and during pricing/margin review.

## Top-level product lanes

### 1. Ready-Made Prints

Products already printed and available now.

Use for:

- inventory already on hand
- market/event items
- quick local pickup
- photo-ready products
- limited quantity items

### 2. Made-to-Order Prints

Standard products that can be printed after purchase or request.

Use for:

- known designs
- repeatable files
- color/size variants
- predictable print time
- products with known cost and margin

### 3. Custom Print Requests

Quote-based products based on customer ideas, photos, dimensions, or needs.

Use for:

- one-off gifts
- replacement-style parts
- unusual sizes
- customer-provided references
- personalized items
- anything that requires review before pricing

### 4. Business / Bulk Orders

Larger or repeated jobs for businesses, events, groups, or organizations.

Use for:

- branded items
- signs
- displays
- event gifts
- bulk custom items
- repeat production work

## Suggested website categories

### Drinkware Accessories

Examples:

- 12 oz can koozies
- cup holders
- bottle accessories
- custom drinkware holders

### Cases and Holders

Examples:

- chapstick cases
- small product cases
- storage holders
- desk organizers
- wallet-style prints

### Lamps and Lighting

Examples:

- printed lamps
- shade pieces
- LED display items
- decorative light structures

Review note:

- Lamps, lighting, wiring, heat, and electrical use require safety review before listing.

### Clocks and Wall Decor

Examples:

- clocks
- signs
- wall pieces
- decorative plaques

### Home Decor

Examples:

- vases
- sculptures
- shelf decor
- themed decor
- seasonal decor

### Gifts and Personalized Items

Examples:

- name-based items
- themed gifts
- holiday items
- pet or hobby items
- personal symbols or phrases

### Practical Parts and Utility Prints

Examples:

- brackets
- holders
- replacement-style parts
- hooks
- tool/desk/shop organizers

Review note:

- Medical, automotive, safety-critical, and load-bearing parts should not be listed as standard products unless the client has verified the material, design, use case, and limits.

### Business and Event Prints

Examples:

- branded items
- table displays
- signage
- giveaways
- bulk gifts

## Product classification fields

Every product should be tagged with:

- Product lane: ready-made, made-to-order, custom, or bulk
- Website category
- Material
- Print time
- Current price
- Estimated cost
- Margin
- Status: active, draft, custom-only, paused, retired

## First category priority

Do not promote every category equally at launch.

Recommended first focus:

1. Best current sellers
2. Products with good margin
3. Products with strong photos
4. Products that are easy to explain
5. Products that can be made reliably

## Do not add yet

Avoid launching too many categories before the client has cost, photo, and production data. Too many weak categories will make the site feel scattered.
