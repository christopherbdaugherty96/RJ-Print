# Product Approval Workflow

Status: launch workflow document

## Purpose

Provide a repeatable process for moving products from idea stage to Shopify-ready and launch-ready status.

## Workflow

Product submitted

↓

Product data collected

↓

Product entered into `data/product_inventory_template.csv`

↓

Photos reviewed

↓

Category assigned

↓

Price reviewed

↓

Cost and margin reviewed

↓

Safety/risk review completed if needed

↓

Shopify fields completed

↓

Policy considerations reviewed

↓

Client approval received

↓

Ready for Shopify setup

↓

Ready for launch review

## Hold conditions

Do not move a product forward if:

- Product name is unclear
- Product photos are missing
- Price is unknown
- Material is unknown
- Print time is unknown
- Product category is unknown
- High-risk review is required and incomplete
- Client approval is missing

## Launch rule

A product should not be treated as launch-ready until all required information is collected and approved.
