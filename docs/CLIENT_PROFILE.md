# RJ Print Client Profile

Status: planning baseline

## Client

RJ Print

## Project type

Client website + business planning support.

## Managed through

Auralis Digital website-business workflow.

## Business summary

RJ Print is a 3D print shop that sells ready-made and custom 3D printed products. The business already has sales activity, but needs clearer structure around product categories, pricing, margins, custom requests, and website presentation.

## Current known problem

The client is selling what they print, but does not yet have a clear system for calculating true product cost, profit margin, product focus, or custom quote rules.

## Main project goal

Create a website and operating plan that turns scattered 3D print sales into a structured business with clear product lanes, margin-aware pricing, and a customer-friendly request process.

## Customer-facing lanes

1. Ready-Made Prints
2. Made-to-Order Prints
3. Custom Print Requests
4. Business / Bulk Orders

## Primary customer actions

- Shop available products.
- Request a custom print quote.
- Ask about made-to-order items.
- Contact RJ Print for business or bulk work.

## Business questions still open

- What are the current best-selling products?
- Which products are already made and ready to sell?
- Which products are made after order?
- Which products should be quote-only?
- What are the current prices?
- What are the real costs and margins?
- Does the client want local pickup, shipping, or both?
- Does the client have product photos, logo, colors, and brand assets?
- Does the client want Shopify checkout immediately or quote/contact first?

## Boundary

Do not build advanced automation before business basics are defined. Printer automation, Shopify-to-printer workflows, and custom backend checkout are later-phase items.
