# RJ Print Legal Tax And Insurance Checklist

Status: planning checklist / requires client confirmation

## Purpose

Track the legal, tax, banking, and insurance questions RJ Print should resolve before public checkout or mature e-commerce launch.

This is not legal, tax, or insurance advice. It is a collection checklist for client decisions and professional follow-up.

## Legal structure

- [ ] Confirm whether RJ Print is currently operating as an individual, sole proprietorship, LLC, partnership, or other entity.
- [ ] Confirm whether the public name and legal name are the same.
- [ ] Confirm who owns the business.
- [ ] Confirm who can approve website copy, product prices, policies, and launch.
- [ ] Confirm whether local permits, zoning, or home-business rules apply.

## EIN and tax setup

- [ ] Confirm whether RJ Print has an EIN.
- [ ] Confirm whether state business tax registration is complete.
- [ ] Confirm whether sales tax registration/license is complete.
- [ ] Confirm sales tax collection method if online checkout is used.
- [ ] Confirm who files sales tax returns.
- [ ] Confirm how income and expenses are tracked.
- [ ] Confirm whether an accountant/bookkeeper is involved.

## Banking and payment processing

- [ ] Confirm business bank account status.
- [ ] Confirm payment method for local sales.
- [ ] Confirm payment method for custom quotes.
- [ ] Confirm payment processor for website checkout if used.
- [ ] Confirm deposit requirement for custom work.
- [ ] Confirm refund/replacement payment workflow.

## Insurance review

- [ ] Confirm general liability insurance status.
- [ ] Confirm product liability coverage status.
- [ ] Confirm home-based business coverage if operating from home.
- [ ] Confirm business property/equipment coverage if needed.
- [ ] Confirm whether business/bulk orders change insurance needs.
- [ ] Confirm whether lamps/electrical products require additional review.
- [ ] Confirm whether children's items, food-contact items, or safety-related parts are avoided or specially reviewed.

## Records to collect

- [ ] Business formation document if any.
- [ ] EIN confirmation if any.
- [ ] State sales tax/license confirmation if any.
- [ ] Insurance declarations if any.
- [ ] Bank/payment processor setup notes.
- [ ] Supplier/material receipts.
- [ ] Product cost records.
- [ ] Quote approvals and payment records.

## Launch rule

If RJ Print uses only a contact/quote website, some legal/tax/payment items may be confirmed before paid transactions happen. If RJ Print enables online checkout, sales tax, payment, shipping, refund, and fulfillment workflows must be verified before launch.
