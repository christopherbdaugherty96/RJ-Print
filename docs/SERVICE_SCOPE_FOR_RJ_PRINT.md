# RJ Print Service Scope

Status: active handoff / scope clarification

## Purpose

Clarify what the builder can handle for RJ Print and what RJ Print must provide before Shopify setup and launch.

This file is meant to make the handoff easier for the client. It separates service work from client-provided information.

## What the builder can handle

The builder can help organize and prepare the Shopify storefront and launch materials.

Builder-side work may include:

- Shopify store structure planning
- Shopify collection planning
- Product catalog organization
- Product page setup using approved product data
- Product descriptions using confirmed facts only
- Basic SEO fields for approved products
- Product photo placement once photos are provided
- Variant/options setup once options are confirmed
- Inventory entry once counts are confirmed
- Shipping/pickup structure documentation
- Policy page drafting from approved policy expectations
- Custom request/contact flow setup
- Quote/contact-first flow planning
- Checkout readiness checklist
- Test checkout checklist if checkout is enabled
- Launch checklist review
- Handoff notes

## What RJ Print must provide

RJ Print must provide or approve the real business and product information.

Client-side inputs needed:

- Actual launch product list
- Product photos
- Product prices
- Materials used
- Print times / production times
- Labor or handling assumptions
- Inventory counts
- Available colors/options
- Customization options
- Product safety or use limitations
- Pickup/shipping rules
- Return/refund expectations
- Custom order rules
- Payment/deposit expectations
- Business contact information
- Domain decision
- Logo or text-logo approval
- Brand colors if any
- Shopify store access
- Approval of collections, prices, policies, checkout settings, and launch scope

## What is not included yet

These items are not part of the current confirmed scope unless separately approved:

- Printer automation
- Shopify-to-printer automation
- Backend checkout
- Custom order queue software
- Automated quoting
- Advanced file upload workflow
- Product photography
- Legal, tax, or insurance services
- Product engineering certification
- Safety certification
- Guaranteed food-safe, child-safe, medical, automotive, load-bearing, or electrical compliance

## Shopify scope rule

Shopify is the intended sales platform, but Shopify setup should be based on confirmed product and business data.

Do not mark Shopify checkout ready until payment, tax, shipping/pickup, policies, products, and a test order are verified.

## Client approval rule

The builder can organize and prepare the store, but RJ Print must approve:

- Product names
- Product prices
- Product descriptions
- Product photos
- Collections
- Shipping/pickup rules
- Policies
- Custom request rules
- Shopify settings
- Launch timing

## Current next step

RJ Print should provide the first 5-10 launch products with photos, prices, materials, production details, options, and inventory counts.

Those products should be entered into `data/product_inventory_template.csv` before Shopify product setup begins.
