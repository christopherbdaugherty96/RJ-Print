# RJ Print Product Safety And Care Guide

Status: planning baseline

## Purpose

Document product expectations, customer care guidance, and categories that require additional review before sale.

## General 3D print notice

3D printed products may contain visible layer lines, minor surface variation, or small cosmetic differences. These are normal characteristics of the manufacturing process.

## General care guidance

- Avoid excessive heat unless specifically stated.
- Avoid open flame.
- Do not assume dishwasher or microwave safety.
- Follow product-specific instructions when provided.

## Higher-risk categories requiring review

- Children's products
- Food-contact products
- Medical-related products
- Safety-critical parts
- Automotive parts
- Electrical products
- Load-bearing replacement parts

## Product boundary

RJ Print should not imply engineering certification, safety certification, food safety, child safety, or performance guarantees unless specifically verified.

## Launch recommendation

Prioritize lower-risk categories:

- Decor
- Display items
- Koozies and drink accessories that do not contact beverages
- Cases and holders
- Desk accessories
- Gifts

## Future work

Add material-specific guidance after the client confirms actual materials, printers, and products.
