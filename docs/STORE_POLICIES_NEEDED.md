# RJ Print Store Policies Needed

Status: planning checklist / requires client confirmation

## Purpose

Track the policy pages and customer-facing rules RJ Print should define before public launch, especially if checkout or paid custom work is enabled.

## Required or strongly recommended policies

- [ ] Shipping / Pickup Policy
- [ ] Return / Refund / Replacement Policy
- [ ] Custom Order Policy
- [ ] Product Safety and Care Policy
- [ ] Privacy Policy
- [ ] Terms of Service
- [ ] Copyright / Trademark / Customer File Policy
- [ ] Local Pickup Policy if pickup is offered

## Shipping / Pickup Policy

Must confirm:

- [ ] Whether RJ Print offers local pickup.
- [ ] Whether RJ Print ships orders.
- [ ] Shipping area: local, U.S., or international.
- [ ] Processing time for ready-made products.
- [ ] Processing time for made-to-order products.
- [ ] Timeline rule for custom orders.
- [ ] Who pays shipping.
- [ ] What happens if a package is damaged.
- [ ] Whether tracking is provided.

## Return / Refund / Replacement Policy

Must confirm:

- [ ] Whether ready-made products can be returned.
- [ ] Whether custom products are final sale.
- [ ] What qualifies as damaged, defective, or incorrect.
- [ ] Whether normal 3D print layer lines are excluded from defect claims.
- [ ] Photo proof requirements for damaged items.
- [ ] Refund, remake, or store-credit options.

## Custom Order Policy

Must confirm:

- [ ] Custom requests are reviewed before acceptance.
- [ ] Quotes must be approved before production.
- [ ] Deposit/payment rules.
- [ ] Revision limits if design work is included.
- [ ] Customer file/reference requirements.
- [ ] Decline/refusal rules for unsafe, infringing, or unrealistic requests.
- [ ] Timeline expectations.

## Product Safety and Care Policy

Must include:

- [ ] General 3D print variation notice.
- [ ] Heat warning.
- [ ] Dishwasher/microwave warning where relevant.
- [ ] Food-contact disclaimer unless specifically verified.
- [ ] Child safety disclaimer where relevant.
- [ ] Product-specific care notes.

## Privacy Policy

Needed if the website collects:

- [ ] Name.
- [ ] Email.
- [ ] Phone.
- [ ] Custom request details.
- [ ] Reference links/files.
- [ ] Checkout or payment information through a third-party platform.

## Terms of Service

Should define:

- [ ] Website/store usage rules.
- [ ] Product availability limits.
- [ ] Order acceptance rules.
- [ ] Custom request review requirement.
- [ ] Limitation of unsupported guarantees.
- [ ] Policy update rights.

## Launch rule

Do not enable public checkout until the relevant policy pages are published and reviewed. A quote/contact-first website may launch with simpler expectation pages, but shipping, pickup, refund, custom order, and safety/care expectations should not be vague.
