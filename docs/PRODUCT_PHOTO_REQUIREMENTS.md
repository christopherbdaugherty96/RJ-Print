# Product Photo Requirements

Status: launch support document

## Purpose

Define the minimum photo requirements for products that will be listed on Shopify.

Good product photos increase trust and reduce confusion.

## Minimum recommended photos per product

### 1. Primary product photo

Requirements:

- Product clearly visible
- Clean background
- Good lighting
- Entire product visible

### 2. Angle photo

Requirements:

- Different viewing angle
- Shows depth and shape

### 3. Detail photo

Requirements:

- Shows texture, layer lines, engraving, or custom details

### 4. Scale photo

Requirements:

- Shows product next to a common object or ruler
- Helps customers understand size

### 5. Option photo

Requirements:

- Shows color or customization options when available

## Recommended extras

- Lifestyle photo
- Product in use photo
- Packaging photo
- Custom personalization example

## File guidelines

- Use clear filenames
- Avoid blurry photos
- Avoid cluttered backgrounds
- Avoid screenshots when possible

## Launch rule

Products may be held back from launch if:

- No usable primary photo exists
- Product size is unclear
- Color options are unclear
- Product quality cannot be reasonably evaluated from the photos
