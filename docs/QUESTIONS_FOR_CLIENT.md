# Questions For RJ Print

Status: planning baseline

Use these questions before finalizing website copy, product pages, pricing, or checkout/request flow.

## Business basics

- What is the exact public business name?
- Do you have a logo?
- Do you have preferred colors or style?
- Do you have a tagline?
- What city/area do you serve?
- Do you want to present as local-only, online, or both?
- What contact email and phone should be public?
- Do you want customers to call, message, submit a form, or buy online first?

## Products

- What products do you currently sell?
- What are your top 5 sellers?
- What products do people ask about most?
- What products are already printed and ready to sell?
- What products are made after order?
- Which products should be custom quote only?
- Which products are hardest to print?
- Which products fail most often?
- Which products take the longest?
- Which products have the best profit?
- Which products do you want featured on the homepage?

## Pricing and costs

- What is the current sale price for each product?
- What filament/material is used for each product?
- How much does the material cost per spool or unit?
- How much material does each product use?
- How long does each product take to print?
- How much labor/finishing time is needed?
- Do you package products?
- What does packaging cost?
- Do you ship products?
- Who pays shipping?
- What payment/platform fees apply?
- Do you want prices visible or quote-based for some products?

## Production

- What printer(s) do you use?
- What materials can you print with?
- What colors do you normally stock?
- What colors are special order?
- What is normal turnaround time?
- What is rush turnaround time, if any?
- What causes delays?
- Do you offer finishing, sanding, painting, or assembly?
- How do you handle failed prints?

## Custom requests

- Do you accept custom designs?
- Can customers send photos?
- Can customers send 3D model files?
- Can you design/model items from scratch?
- What custom requests do you want to avoid?
- Do you require a deposit for custom work?
- How many revisions or approvals are included?
- Do you want a file upload form now or later?

## Shipping and pickup

- Do you offer local pickup?
- Where/how is pickup arranged?
- Do you ship locally, nationally, or both?
- What shipping carriers do you use?
- Who pays shipping?
- What is the return/refund policy?
- What happens if an item breaks during shipping?

## Website content

- Do you have product photos?
- Do you have videos of prints being made?
- Do you have customer photos or reviews?
- Do you have social media accounts?
- Do you want Instagram/TikTok embedded or linked?
- Do you want a gallery page?
- Do you want online checkout immediately or quote/contact first?

## Approval

- Who approves website copy?
- Who approves product prices?
- Who approves product photos?
- Who approves launch?
- What date would you like the first version ready?

## Final question

If the website could help RJ Print with one thing first, what matters most: more product sales, more custom requests, better organization, or better trust?
