## FAQ draft

### How long does a print take?

Print time depends on the size, material, complexity, and current order queue. Custom requests are reviewed before timing is confirmed.

### Can I request a custom design?

Yes. Send your idea, photo, measurements, or reference and RJ Print will review whether it can be printed and quoted.

### Can I choose colors?

Color options depend on available materials and the product type. Some colors may require special order or extra time.

### Do you offer pickup or shipping?

Pickup and shipping options should be confirmed before launch. Add the final policy here once the client decides.

### Can you make replacement parts?

RJ Print may review replacement-style part requests, but fit, strength, heat resistance, food safety, and other performance requirements must be discussed before approval. No engineering or safety guarantee should be implied unless specifically verified.

### Do custom requests require approval before payment?

Custom requests should be reviewed and quoted before production. Payment or deposit rules should be confirmed by the client.
