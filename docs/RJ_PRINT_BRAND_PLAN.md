# RJ Print Brand Plan

## Final decision

RJ Print will begin as a 3D-print product line inside Auralis Digital.

RJ Print will use:

- Auralis Digital website
- Auralis Digital Shopify store
- Existing domain
- Existing checkout and payment systems
- Existing ecommerce workflows

RJ Print should not receive a separate website at this stage.

## Purpose

RJ Print is focused on custom and ready-made 3D-printed products.

Initial categories include:

- Koozies
- Lamps
- Clocks
- Wallets
- Home Decor
- Desk Accessories
- Custom 3D Prints
- Ready-to-Ship Prints
- Seasonal Prints

## Storefront placement

RJ Print products will be listed under:

```text
Products
└── 3D Prints
```

within the Auralis Digital storefront.

Recommended public wording:

> RJ Print is the 3D-print collection by Auralis Digital, featuring custom and ready-made 3D-printed products.

## Business goal

The goal is to:

- Validate demand
- Build product listings
- Generate sales
- Keep operations simple
- Avoid duplicate websites and systems

before considering a standalone RJ Print business site.

## Future path

RJ Print may later become:

- Its own domain
- Its own Shopify store
- Its own subdomain
- Its own standalone brand

Only if sales volume, brand demand, or operational complexity justify the change.

## Relationship to Auralis Digital

Auralis Digital remains the umbrella business.

RJ Print is a featured product line operating within that ecosystem.

Rule:

```text
Auralis Digital = umbrella brand
RJ Print = 3D-print product line
3D Prints = category under Products
```
